# Base Lore

This game is set in an American suburban elementary school and community. The protagonist is a fifth-grade boy, an only child. The daily rhythm flows from morning at home, to school, after school, evening in the community, and night back home. The story revolves around friendship, dealing with bullying, family support, and personal growth.

---

## School Background: Pinewood Elementary

### About the School
- Located in a quiet suburban community with about 450 students
- Founded 30 years ago, renovated once
- School motto: "Care, Respect, Growth"
- Has a comprehensive anti-bullying policy and counseling system

### School Rules
- No running in hallways, keep quiet
- School hours: 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM
- Lunch time: 12:00 PM to 12:45 PM
- Students can seek help from teachers or counselors at any time
- The school encourages reporting inappropriate behavior and promises to protect reporters

### Class 5A
- Homeroom teacher: Ms. Carter
- Class size: 24 students
- Classroom located in the middle of the second floor hallway
- Overall friendly atmosphere, but a few students bully others
- Current activities: Science fair preparation, reading program

## Themes and Educational Focus

### Core Values
1. **Asking for help is not weakness**: Seeking adult help when facing problems is the right thing to do
2. **The power of empathy**: Understanding that others may also have difficulties
3. **Building support networks**: Good friends can support each other
4. **Recording and reporting**: Write down what happens so adults can understand the situation
5. **Safety first**: Never use violence to solve problems

### Correct Ways to Handle Bullying
- Stay calm, don't provoke the other person
- Walk away and find a safe place
- Tell a trusted adult (parents, teachers, counselor)
- Record the time, place, and what happened
- Stay connected with friends, don't isolate yourself
- Believe things will get better

### Prohibited Content
- Violent retaliation or harming others
- Thoughts or behaviors of self-harm
- Cyberbullying or spreading rumors
- Discriminatory language

## Time System

### Daily Schedule
- **Morning**: Breakfast at home, prepare for school
- **School Morning**: Arrive at school, attend classes
- **Lunch**: Eat in cafeteria, free time
- **School Afternoon**: Continue classes, dismissal
- **Evening**: Activities in the community, spend time with friends
- **Night**: Return home, dinner, spend time with family

### Weekly Plan
- Game time spans one semester
- Each week has specific events and challenges
- Special activities available on weekends
- Main quests progress with time

---

## Game Objectives

During this semester, the protagonist needs to:
1. Build true friendships
2. Learn to face and handle bullying
3. Develop confidence and social skills
4. Maintain good family relationships
5. Help others who need help

The ultimate goal is to create a friendlier classroom environment while growing into a better version of yourself in the process.
